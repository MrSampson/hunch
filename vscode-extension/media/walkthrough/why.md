# Why is this the way it is?

Open any source file:

- **Hover** a symbol to see its bug history and fragility.
- The **status bar** shows how many invariants guard the active file — click it
  for the full brief.

Run **Hunch: Why is this?** from the editor title bar, the right-click menu, or
the Command Palette for the full brief — invariants, decisions (and what they
rejected), bug history, and the blast radius of changing this file. With the
cursor on a symbol, the brief opens on that symbol's history first.
