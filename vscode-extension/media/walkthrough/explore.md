# Open the Hunch view

Hunch is your repo's **engineering memory** — a curated graph of *why* the code
is the way it is: decisions, invariants, bug history, and fragility.

Click the **🧠 Hunch** icon in the Activity Bar to browse:

- **Invariants** — rules the codebase must not break
- **Decisions** — why things are the way they are
- **Bugs** & **Bug lineage** — what broke before, and what keeps coming back
- **Fragile symbols** — churn + bug + fan-in hotspots
- **Stale records** — facts whose guarded files changed since they were verified

The view appears automatically in any repo that has a committed `.hunch/` folder.
